# DUAL_NETWORK_EXPERIMENT_LOG

## 0. Branch Position

This log records the `feature/dual-network-variational` branch.

The branch explores a dual-network variational inversion path:

- `phi-Net` reconstructs a scalar field `phi(x, y)`;
- `mu-Net` predicts a permeability field `mu(x, y)`;
- `phi` is trained through field/data terms;
- `mu` is updated through weak-form material residuals and optional priors.

This branch does not replace `main`. The strongest current results use BCE mask supervision from `mu_label < 500`, so they are semi-supervised / diagnostic upper-bound results, not proof of unsupervised weak-form inversion success.

## Step S7: Area Prior Probe

Purpose:

- Test whether a soft area prior can suppress full-domain low-`mu` collapse.

Configuration:

- Reused S3 `.npz`
- `outer_steps=30`, `phi_steps=30`, `mu_steps=30`
- `test_radius=5.0`, `center_mode=three`
- Tested `lambda_area_prior=0.0`, `0.1`, `1.0`

Key results:

- `lambda_area_prior=1.0` reduced final `defect_area_pred` from `200` to `77`.
- `mu_mse` and `mu_mae` improved versus no area prior.
- Final IoU remained low: `7.317073e-02`.

Current judgment:

- Area prior helps suppress collapse but does not solve localization.
- The target area comes from `mu_label`, so this is already a diagnostic supervised signal, not a final unsupervised formulation.

## Step S8: Stronger Area Prior Probe

Purpose:

- Test whether stronger area prior values further improve area and localization.

Configuration:

- Reused S3 `.npz`
- Fixed `test_radius=5.0`, `center_mode=three`
- Tested `lambda_area_prior=1.0`, `3.0`, `10.0`

Key results:

- Final `defect_area_pred`: `77` at `lambda=1.0`, `50` at `lambda=3.0`, `41` at `lambda=10.0`.
- Stronger area prior improved area suppression and `mu_mse/mu_mae`.
- IoU dropped to `0.0` for `lambda=3.0` and `10.0`.

Current judgment:

- Strong area prior controls area but can miss the defect location entirely.
- The bottleneck shifts from area collapse to localization.

## Step S9: Localization Diagnostics

Purpose:

Configuration:

- Reused S3 `.npz`
- Compared `lambda_area_prior=0.0` and `1.0`
- Saved final `mu_pred`, `mu_label`, masks, diagnostics, and PNGs.

Key results:

- No area prior: `defect_area_pred=200`, `defect_iou=5.500000e-02`, full-domain low-`mu` collapse.
- `lambda_area_prior=1.0`: `defect_area_pred=77`, `defect_iou=7.317073e-02`.
- Label centroid: `(-7.248804, 4.040404)`.
- Area-prior prediction centroid: `(-2.388927, 6.536796)`.

Current judgment:

- Area prior reduces collapse but leaves a broad, shifted low-`mu` band.
- Next useful direction is localization information, not simply stronger area prior.

## Step S12: Lightweight Mask Prior

Purpose:

- Add a soft Dice mask prior as a diagnostic local positioning signal.

Configuration:

- Reused S3 `.npz`
- `lambda_area_prior=1.0`, `test_radius=5.0`, `center_mode=three`
- Tested `lambda_mask_prior=0.0`, `0.01`, `0.1`, `1.0`

Key results:

- `lambda_mask_prior=1.0` improved final IoU from `7.317073e-02` to `1.666667e-01`.
- Final `defect_area_pred` decreased from `77` to `66`.
- `mu_mse` and `mu_mae` improved versus no mask prior.

Current judgment:

- Soft Dice mask prior helps, but predicted area remains far larger than label area `11`.
- The branch is missing a stronger false-positive suppression / localization mechanism.
