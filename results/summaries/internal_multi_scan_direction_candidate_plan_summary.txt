- delta_b shape: `(N,3,2,9,201)`
- axis 1: `[Bx, By, Bz]`
- axis 2: `direction_names=[x_scan,y_scan]`

- axis 4: path samples

direction metadata: ["direction_mask","scan_line_mask","direction_names","path_coordinate_axis","line_coordinate_axis","path_coordinate_m","line_coordinate_m","sensor_z_m","observation_variant","base_group_id"]

- D0_reference_single_direction: fit_for_23_2b=False, rows/base=0, decision=reuse_existing

- D2_dual_direction_9line_z0p008: fit_for_23_2b=True, rows/base=1, decision=default_generate_y_scan
