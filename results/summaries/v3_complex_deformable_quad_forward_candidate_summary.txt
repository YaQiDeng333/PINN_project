## Method

* Geometry head: BzEncoder -> coarse rotated box + 4 bounded corner residual offsets.
* Residual scale is fixed at `0.35` of width/height. No residual-scale search is used.
* Rasterizer: pure PyTorch soft quadrilateral half-plane rasterizer with fixed temperature `0.08`.
* Loss: BCEWithLogits + soft Dice + `0.1` * frozen mask-to-Bz surrogate MSE.
* No SDF loss, boundary head, vertex supervision, polygon-vertex count prediction, retrieval, post-processing, lambda search, or temperature search is used.
