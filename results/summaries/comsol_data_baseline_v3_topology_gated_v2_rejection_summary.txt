## Protocol

## Review Outcome

Claude review found no data leakage, metadata-input violation, true-label hard routing, normalization issue, checkpoint/test leakage, or baseline document overwrite.

The review did identify must-fix performance/method issues:

- cc2 did not improve and regressed versus 20.44 gated v1.
- residual adapters contributed too weakly to affect the output.
- source_pack differences should be investigated before any further V3 promotion attempt.

These are not safe in-place fixes under the fixed 20.45 POC rules, because addressing them would require a new method or controlled hyperparameter experiment. This run is therefore recorded as a rejected POC rather than repaired in place.
