## Conclusion

Composite-selection improves the main mask/shape metrics at threshold=500: IoU and Dice increase, area_error decreases, and pred_area=0 decreases compared with CURRENT_BASELINE.
MSE and MAE are worse than CURRENT_BASELINE and must be recorded as the trade-off, but the degradation is below the >15% severe-cost threshold used in this gate.
Threshold=600 is diagnostic only: IoU and Dice are higher, but area_error is worse than the threshold=500 composite candidate result, so it is not used for baseline update.
Acceptance condition satisfied: True. Composite-selection is a formal candidate record, but CURRENT_BASELINE is not changed in this step.
