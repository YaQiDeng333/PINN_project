## Judgment

Composite-selection improvement is sample-broad for IoU/Dice rather than only a few outliers.
Small defects improve for a majority of samples by IoU, and pred_area=0 repairs are concentrated in the small bin.
Composite-selection increases predicted area on average, which partially mitigates the baseline area underestimation.
MSE / MAE cost mainly comes from increased background-region error; defect-region MAE decreases instead. This means composite-selection is biased toward improving defect shape and defect-region prediction while sacrificing part of the background numeric accuracy.
This audit supports keeping composite-selection as a formal candidate record. It does not by itself update CURRENT_BASELINE.
