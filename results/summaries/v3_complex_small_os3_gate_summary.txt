## Judgment

Small defect metrics did not improve overall: small pred_area=0 increased and small IoU/Dice decreased.
Overall gate does not satisfy acceptance: IoU/Dice decreased and area_error increased.
The oversampled model did not push the problem into area overestimation; pred_area>true decreased, but pred_area=0 and pred_area<true increased, so underestimation became worse.
Conclusion: stop this oversampling gate at seed=42 and do not expand to 3 seeds.
